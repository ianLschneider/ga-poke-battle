PokéBattle

Welcome to PokéBattle – A simple Pokémon battle game.

Once a game has begun, players will have 60 seconds to pick a move and battle their opponent. After each move players will alternate turns and the play clock will reset to 60 seconds. The first player to reach 0 health points looses.
