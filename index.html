PokéBattle

Welcome to PokéBattle – A simple Pokémon battle game.

Once a battle has begun players will trade off turns, one after the other. Each turn will have a 60 second play limit. If the player takes longer than 60 seconds to pick a move, that player will forfeit their turn.
